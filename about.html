About FDU Hospital

About FDU Hospital FDU Hospital is the universities standalone hospital located at 1000 River Rd in Teaneck, NJ. The hospital is available for FDU students, faculty, and staff who are experiencing health problems or have appointments with the hospital and visitors are only limited to relatives having a business with FDU. FDU Hospital is the #1 best college hospital in New Jersey.
